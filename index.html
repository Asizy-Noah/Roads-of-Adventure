Roads of Adventure Safaris

Roads of Adventure Safaris is one of the most trusted and leading Africa safari tour operators, specializing in gorilla trekking experiences in Uganda and Rwanda, as well as thrilling wildlife safaris across Kenya, Tanzania, Botswana, Zambia, South Africa, Namibia, Zimbabwe, and Madagascar.

We craft exceptional primate tour packages that include gorilla trekking, chimpanzee tracking, and combined wildlife safaris. Our diverse offerings feature birding safaris, honeymoon safaris, family safaris, Budget safaris, Luxury safaris, group tours, game drive safaris, Big five safaris, Africa big cats safaris, Masai Mara safaris, Serengeti safaris, beach holidays, mountaineering, hot air balloon safaris, , photography safaris, filming safaris, walking safaris, camping safaris, canoeing safaris, wildebeest migration safaris, boat excursions, Mokoro tours, cultural tours, and unforgettable trips to Victoria Falls.

Join us for extraordinary adventures in iconic locations such as Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, Kibale forest park, Murchison falls national park, Masai Mara National Reserve, Serengeti National Park, Kilimanjaro National Park, Zanzibar Island, Diani Beach, Kruger National Park, Chobe National Park, Okavango Delta, Etosha National Park, Hwange National Park, and South Luangwa National Park.
