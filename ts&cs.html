2. Booking and Payment

To confirm a booking, a deposit of 30% of the total safari cost is required. The remaining balance must be paid 60 days prior to the safari start date. For bookings made within 60 days of departure, full payment is required at the time of booking.

Payments can be made via bank transfer, credit card, or other methods as specified during the booking process. All bank charges and transfer fees are the responsibility of the client.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation of a confirmed booking must be made in writing. Cancellation charges are as follows:

  • More than 60 days before departure: 10% of total safari cost
  • 59-45 days before departure: 25% of total safari cost
  • 44-30 days before departure: 50% of total safari cost
  • 29-15 days before departure: 75% of total safari cost
  • 14 days or less before departure: 100% of total safari cost

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that includes cancellation coverage.

4. Changes to Bookings

If you wish to make changes to your booking, please contact us as soon as possible. We will make every effort to accommodate your request, but changes cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional costs.

Roads of Adventure Safaris reserves the right to make changes to itineraries if necessary due to circumstances beyond our control, such as weather conditions, road closures, or safety concerns. In such cases, we will provide alternatives of equal or greater value whenever possible.

It is the client's responsibility to ensure they have valid passports, visas, and any other required travel documents for all countries visited. Passports should be valid for at least six months beyond the return date.

Clients are also responsible for obtaining any necessary vaccinations and health certificates. Roads of Adventure Safaris cannot be held responsible for any issues arising from inadequate travel documentation.

6. Travel Insurance

Comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory for all clients participating in our safaris. Your insurance should cover medical expenses, emergency evacuation, repatriation, cancellation, curtailment, and loss or damage to personal belongings.

Proof of insurance must be provided prior to the safari start date. Roads of Adventure Safaris reserves the right to refuse participation if adequate insurance is not in place.
